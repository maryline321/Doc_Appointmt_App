About us

The Sea Park Hospital is a 102 bed multispecialty Hospital in Kenya
located in Karen along Karen Langata Road, Nairobi.
We offer patient focused, world class medical treatment and accord the highest standards of
medical care to all our patients indiscriminately. With our State of the art amenities,
equipment and our well trained staff, we are committed to delivering the most
effective and most compassionate care to all our patients. Since inception in 2006,
The Sea Park Hospital has been acclaimed for excellence in clinical care in Africa and beyond.
The Sea Park Hospital proud to be ISO 9001:2015 certified for compliance with Quality Management Systems
and is committed to continuous improvement in service delivery. In a bid to bring quality and affordable
medical care closer to the people, The Sea Park Hospital offers expertise and treatment
options conveniently located in different parts of Kenya in its 8 branches.
